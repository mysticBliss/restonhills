Our Program

Day 1: Arrival Srinagar
On arrival at Srinagar Airport you will meet by our Representative Cum Driver and transfer you to Hotel Check in Hotel, After Fresh Start Visiting of Shrines In Srinagar like, Dastageer Sahab, Rozabal Shrine, Khawaja Bazar Khawja Syed Moin-ud-Din Naqshbandi, Jamia Masjid, Shah Hamdan Mosque, In the Evening Back to Hotel For Overnight Rest.

Day 2: Sonmarg Day Trip
This Morning after Breakfast Drive to Hazratbal Shrine, which is situated on the left bank of the famous Dal Lake in Srinagar this unmatched reverence is anchored in the love and respect for Prophet Mohammad(Peace be upon him),whose Moi-e-Muqaddas,(The Scared Hair or Holy Relic)is Preserved Here. and continue drive to Sonmarg Full day enjoyment in Sonmarg. Sonmarg is Known as the Gate way of Ladakh, in the evening return Back to Srinagar For Overnight Stay.

Day 3: Gulmarg Day Trip
This Morning after Breakfast Drive to Baba Reshi Shrine. It is named after Sufi Saint Baba Payam Uddin Reshi, The Shrine of Baba Reshi has Become a Popular Gulmarg Tourist Place to Visit and is thronged by visitors throughout the year. Jammu and Kashmir Tourism Department also Recognizes this holy Shrines as a virtual Attraction of the Valley. and Continue Drive to Gulmarg Full Day enjoyment in Gulmarg. which is one of the most beautiful Summer Destination in the Valley. On arrival at gulmarg once can a gondola ride(cable Car) From Gulmarg to Khalinmarg Enjoy the Natural Beauty of Gulmarg, In the evening Come back to Srinagar for overnight stay.

Day 4: Srinagar to Pahalgam
This Morning After Breakfast drive to Pahalgam On the way go to Aishmuqam, it is the Shrine of a Sufi Saint Baba Zaina-ud-din wali, who was Commonly Known by the Name "Zaino Shah Sahib". This Shrine is Located on a Hillock, which stands 86 Km away from Srinagar en Route to Pahalgam. The Main Sanctorum is Placed Inside a deep and a Narrow cave, Which also Contains Several Relics Associated to Baba in a room. One Such relic is "the Rod of Staff"of Prophet Moses PUBH. and continue drive to Pahalgam Reach Pahalgam Check In Hotel, after fresh Enjoy the Natural Beauty of Pahalgam Evening back to Hotel For overnight Rest.

Day 5: Pahalgam- Srinagar
This Morning After Breakfast you can hire a local taxi only for sightseeing at Pahalgam Like, Chandanwari, Aru Valley & Betab Valley Outside txi are not allowed to take tourists to different Tourist Spots. Evening Come Back to Srinagar for Overnight Stay.

Day 6: Srinagar Local Sightseeing
This Morning after breakfast start Local Sightseeing of Mughal Gardens Visiting The Nishat Bagh (The Garden of Pleasure) & Shalimar Bagh(Adobe of Love) & the Chashma Shahi Garden & In the evening Boat ride (Shikara Ride) around Dal Lake Evening Come Back to Hotel For Overnight Rest.

Day 7: Departure Srinagar Airport
This Morning after breakfast Drive to Srinagar Airport to Connect the Flight at own Words Destination. Thus The Package Ended with sweet Memories of Kashmir.
