Our Program

Day-1: AIRPORT TO LEH
Arrival Leh Kushok Bakula Airport (This must be one of the Most Sensational Flights in The World. The peaks of K2, Nanga Parbat, Gasherbrum and Nun Kun massif can be seen at a distance from the other side of the plane, sometimes it’s so close that you feel that you could reach out and touch it.) On the arrival at the airport, you will be received by our representative and transfer to Hotel for Check in. This date will be spend with complete rest and leisure to acclimatize followed by Welcome tea or Coffee at the Hotel. Evening Visit to Shantis stupa & Leh Palace. Dinner & Overnight at Hotel.

Day-2: Leh Sham Valley Tour
Full day of visit, after Breakfast drive 40 Kms (3 to 4 hours) visit Spituk Monastery, Kali Mata, Pather Sahib, Magnetic hill, Confluence of Zanskar - Indus river and Hall of fame. Overnight Hotel.

Day-03: Pangong Lake Day Trip
Full day trip, after early Breakfast leave for Pangong Lake drive 154 Kms (7 to 8 hours) through Changla Pass (5300 Mtrs,17,350fts) one of the highest motor able road in the world. Visit the beautiful Pangong Lake forms a part of the border area between India and China. This Large, serene Lake at an altitude of 13,930 fts above the sea level has a brilliant color variation including deep blue, turquoise etc. Enjoy your Packed Lunch on the bank of Lake. Drive back to Leh. Overnight Hotel.

Day-04: Leh Excursion Tour
Full day of visit, after Breakfast drive about 40 Kms (3 to 4 hours) en route visit Sindhu Ghat, Shey, Thiksey and Stok Palace Museum. Overnight Hotel.

Day-05: LEH TO AIRPORT DROP
After Breakfast Transfer to the Airport and fly back to Delhi with Sweet memories of Ladakh.
