Our Program

Day1 : Arrival at Jammu, transfers to Katra (60 kms/ 1.5 hrs)
Pick up from Jammu airport/ railway Station and transfers to Katra. On reaching, check in at hotel, afternoon free for personal leisure. Overnight stay and dinner at hotel.

Day2 : Vaishno Devi - Darshan Day
Morning after breakfast trek / horse ride from Katra to Mata Vaishnodevi Darshan (15.kms one way). Back at Katra for Dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

Day3 : Katra to Patnitop (90 kms/ 3 hrs)
After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfers to Patnitop. Check-in at hotel, refresh yourself & explore the surroundings on your own. Evening return back to hotel for overnight stay.

Day4 : Patnitop to Pahalgam (180kms/ 5 hrs)
After breakfast check out from hotel and transfers to Pahalgam by road. Arrive at Pahalgam, check in at Hotel, day to relax, dinner and overnight stay at Srinagar.

Day5 : Pahalgam city tour
In Pahalgam, visit Betaab Valley and Chandanwari as well as take a horse riding trip to Baisaran meadows.(at your own cost). After sightseeing, rest of the day will be at leisure for individual activities. Overnight in hotel.

Day6 : Pahalgam to Gulmarg (152 kilometers / 4-5 hrs drive)
After breakfast, begin the country tour towards Gulmarg via Tangmarg. Drive ahead on a scenic drive of 14 Kms to Gulmarg. Arrive in Gulmarg early in the afternoon and check in at the hotel. Rest of the day is at leisure. We will stay at the hotel for overnight.

Day7 : Gulmarg city tour
Begin a short tour, boarding the Gondola cable car system (the 08 minutes ropeway). Descend back to Gulmarg after an hour and later indulge in some horse-riding. Dinner and overnight at hotel.

Day8 : Gulmarg to Srinagar (56 kilometers / 2-3 hrs drive)
After breakfast, check out from Gulmarg and drive to Srinagar. Day at leisure. Overnight stay at Hotel/ houseboat.

Day9 : Yusmarg day trip (47 kms/ 2 hrs
After breakfast, set out for an excursion to Yusmarg. Yusmarg, the meadow of Jesus is an apline valley located in Badgam district and covered by snow clad mountains and meadows of pine and fir. This valley mesmerizes tourists with its natural beauty. This is also popular for horse riding, fishing and for skiing during winter. After spending some quality time, return back to Srinagar for overnight stay.

Day10 : Sonmarg day trip (85 kms/ 2 hrs)
After breakfast, drive to Sonmarg (90 kms/3 hrs) enroute Kheer Bhawani Darshan. On arrival at Sonmarg, visit Thajjwas glacier (own cost) . Return back to Srinagar. Dinner and overnight stay in hotel.

Day11 : Srinagar city tour
After breakfast, we proceed for a half day city tour that includes visit to the famous Mughal gardens of Nishat and Shalimar, the Chasme Shahi Garden, Shalimar Bagh (Abode of love) and Shankaracharya Temple. We will explore the gardens and later drive back to the hotel for a dinner and Overnight stay at hotel.

Day12 : Departure/transfers to Srinagar Airport
After your breakfast, we will assist you with transfers to airport. Tour ends with sweet memories of Kashmir
